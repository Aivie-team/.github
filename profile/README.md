## üöÄ About Us

**Aivie** (pronounced "Eye-vee") is an AI-powered Chrome extension that improves real-time communication during online interviews. We combine **AI-driven coaching**, **live translation**, and **feedback analytics** to help talented professionals ‚Äî especially non-native speakers ‚Äî overcome communication barriers, anxiety, and cultural challenges during interviews.

> We believe there are countless skilled technicians and developers who fail interviews not because they lack ability, but because of communication issues, stress, or cultural barriers. **Aivie exists to fix that.**

### Our Mission
- To empower professionals to express their skills confidently
- To break down communication barriers in global interviews
- To provide ethical AI assistance that builds authentic confidence
- To create a supportive ecosystem for career growth and learning

## üí° What We Do

### Core Features

1. **Smart Overlay Console**
   - Real-time assistant overlay for Google Meet, Zoom, and Teams
   - Displays live translation, transcript, and contextual prompts
   - Stealth and Transparent modes available

2. **Real-Time Transcription & Translation**
   - Multi-language speech-to-text and translation
   - Bi-directional understanding for both interviewer and candidate
   - Local processing for privacy-first architecture

3. **Adaptive Interview Coaching**
   - STAR-method response guidance
   - Tone analysis and smart prompts
   - Focus on clarity and confidence, not memorized answers

4. **Coding Interview Support**
   - Detects shared code or IDEs
   - Provides logic suggestions and reasoning improvement
   - Reduces stress during technical interviews

5. **Post-Interview Analysis**
   - Insights on filler usage, pacing, and language clarity
   - Performance tracking and communication heatmaps
   - Targeted improvement suggestions

6. **Privacy & Ethics**
   - Local audio processing whenever possible
   - GDPR & CCPA compliant
   - Transparent AI assistance ‚Äî empowering real talent, not faking expertise
